About Our Lab

Transforming Stem Cell Biology Through Patient Care, Scientific Discovery, and Mentoring

The Ghiaur Lab, housed within the Sidney Kimmel Comprehensive Cancer Center at Johns Hopkins, brings together clinical expertise, mechanistic discovery, and scientific training to advance the understanding of stem cell biology. Our research investigates how the bone marrow microenvironment shapes hematopoietic stem cell homeostasis, stem cell aging, clonal evolution, and malignant transformation. Guided by real-world clinical challenges, we strive to uncover mechanisms that bridge basic biology with therapeutic innovation, ultimately improving outcomes for patients with hematologic diseases. Through this work, we remain committed to training the next generation of leaders in hematopoietic stem cell biology.
